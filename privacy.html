Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect #

a) Information you provide

  • Name, phone number, and email address when you register or join the waitlist.
  • Emergency contacts you designate within the app.
  • Profile details and any content you submit through support channels.

b) Location data

  • Precise GPS location when you trigger an SOS or enable live location sharing.
  • General location used to populate your local directory of police, hospitals, and pharmacies.
  • Location is only transmitted to your designated contacts and relevant authorities during an active emergency event.

c) Device & usage data

  • Device identifiers, operating system version, and app version.
  • Crash logs and anonymised usage analytics to improve reliability.
  • IP address and browser type when you visit our website.

d) Sensor & audio data (optional features)

  • Microphone access is used solely for voice-trigger detection on your device; audio is processed locally and is not stored on our servers.
  • Volume button presses for discreet mode activation are handled on-device only.
settings

2. How We Use Your Information #

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Ally platform and its safety features.
  • Dispatch SOS alerts to your emergency contacts and, where applicable, local first responders.
  • Personalise your experience — such as surfacing relevant nearby resources.
  • Communicate important updates, security notices, and product announcements.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or harmful activity.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

share

3. Sharing Your Information #

We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Emergency contacts — your name, location, and alert status during an active SOS event.
  • First responders & authorities — location and incident data when you or we contact emergency services on your behalf.
  • Service providers — trusted vendors who help us operate our infrastructure (e.g. cloud hosting, analytics), bound by strict data-processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements — when required by applicable law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of any person.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with prior notice to you.
my_location

4. Location Data & Emergency Features #

Because location is central to Ally's safety mission, we treat it with extra care:

  • Precise location is only collected when a safety feature (SOS, live sharing) is actively in use.
  • Background location access, when granted, is used exclusively to enable automatic threat-zone alerts; it is never used for profiling or advertising.
  • You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings. Note that doing so disables core safety functionality.
  • Location history is retained for 30 days to help resolve disputes or investigations, then permanently deleted.
lock

5. Data Security #

We implement industry-standard safeguards including end-to-end encryption for SOS transmissions, TLS for all data in transit, and AES-256 encryption at rest. Access to personal data is restricted to authorised personnel on a need-to-know basis. Despite these measures, no system is completely immune — we encourage you to use a strong, unique password and keep your device software up to date.

schedule

6. Data Retention #

  • Account data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Location history: 30 days.
  • Anonymised analytics: up to 24 months.
  • After account deletion, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights #

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — request erasure of your personal data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection / restriction — object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at team@safetyally.app. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy #

Ally is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU/UK). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child without appropriate consent, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

11. Account Deletion #

How to request deletion

  • Send an email to team@safetyally.app from the email address associated with the account you want to delete.
  • Use the subject line: REQUEST FOR ACCOUNT DELETION.
  • Optionally include any relevant details (e.g. your user ID or phone number) to help us locate your account.

What gets deleted

We delete all data and activity associated with your Ally account, including:

  • Account details — name, email address, and phone number.
  • Emergency contacts and safety-circle data.
  • Location history and SOS event logs.
  • App preferences and notification settings.

Timeline & retention

We will permanently delete all your personal data within 14 working days of receiving and verifying your request. No personal data is retained after deletion, except where we are required by law to keep certain records (e.g. financial or regulatory obligations), in which case we will inform you of the specific retention period.

Confirmation

We will notify you by email once your account and data have been fully deleted.
